Research & Engineering Philosophy

01

I build on robust data quality

Every intelligent system begins with high-fidelity data engineering — standardizing schemas, designing ETL/ELT pipelines, and enforcing lineage. Whether processing streaming meeting transcripts or structuring logs, the model is only as good as the data pipeline supporting it.

02

I ship evaluated, guardrailed AI

Prototyping LLM applications is easy; productionizing them is hard. In Brand Guardian AI, I integrated DeepEval hallucination and citation-coverage metrics directly into the CI/CD pipeline. Shipping LLM guardrails as a deploy-time safety net ensures that system updates remain safe, aligned, and regression-free.

03

I design for real-time scale

AI must feel responsive and alive. By leveraging async orchestration frameworks like Inngest and real-time streaming tools like Twilio WebSockets and Deepgram STT, I build interactive voice and code-generation agents with latencies under 2 seconds. AI delivers true value only when optimized for production performance.
